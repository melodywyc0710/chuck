Activity 7: Extended Analysis — Exam Practice

A7
Marks available10 marks
Look at the design described below. Write an extended analysis (approx. 3–4 sentences) that discusses TWO design elements or principles and ONE gestalt principle. Use the NAME → EVIDENCE → EFFECT formula throughout.

Design to Analyse:

A fundraising poster for a children's hospital charity. The poster features a large, full-bleed photograph of a smiling child's face, cropped tightly at the top of the frame, against a bright orange background. In the lower third, white sans-serif text reads "GIVE HOPE" in bold, followed by a smaller tagline in lighter weight. The charity's logo appears in the bottom right corner, isolated by a generous amount of white space around it.

✅ Sample response (refer to after completing)

"The design element of colour is used through the bright orange background, which creates a warm, energetic tone that conveys optimism and urgency, appropriate for a fundraising context. The principle of cropping is applied to the child's face by cutting the top of the image frame, creating an intimate, close-up framing that draws the viewer's eye directly to the subject's expression. The gestalt principle of hierarchy — established through the large, bold white 'GIVE HOPE' text against the orange ground — signals the primary call-to-action as the most important message, with the tagline and logo at reduced scale establishing secondary reading order."
