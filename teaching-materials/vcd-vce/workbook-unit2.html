Activity 7: Extended Response — Case Study

A7
Marks available10 marks
Read the case study below. Write an extended response (approx. 4–5 sentences) identifying ONE trend and ONE industry connection, and explaining the factors behind each.

Case Study:

A furniture company launches a new modular chair made from recycled ocean plastic. The chair was designed collaboratively between an industrial designer and a materials engineer, and its packaging was created by a separate communication design studio using only recyclable cardboard and soy-based ink. The company also trademarked the chair's distinctive silhouette to prevent competitors from copying its design.

✅ Sample response (refer to after completing)

This case study reflects the sustainability trend, driven primarily by environmental factors — evident in the use of recycled ocean plastic for the chair and recyclable, soy-ink packaging, both responding to growing consumer concern about waste and material sourcing. It also demonstrates industry collaboration, with the industrial designer working alongside a materials engineer to solve the technical challenge of working with recycled plastic, and a separate communication design studio handling the packaging — showing how a single product relies on multiple specialists across different design fields. Finally, the company's decision to trademark the chair's silhouette highlights the role of intellectual property in protecting original design work from being copied once it reaches the market.
